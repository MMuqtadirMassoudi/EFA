About

Attiqulla Abid

Profession: Attiqulla Abid is a dedicated Physics instructor at Education For Afghanistan (EFA). With a deep passion for the sciences and a commitment to educational excellence, he brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to his teaching role.

What I Teach: Attiqulla specializes in teaching Physics, covering fundamental concepts as well as advanced topics. His curriculum includes classical mechanics, electromagnetism, thermodynamics, and modern physics. He is adept at making complex theories accessible and engaging for students of all levels.

Teaching Philosophy: Attiqulla believes in a student-centered approach, where learning is tailored to each individual's needs and interests. He emphasizes interactive and practical learning experiences to help students grasp abstract concepts and apply them effectively.

Experience: With years of experience in both classroom and online teaching environments, Attiqulla has developed a reputation for his clear explanations and supportive teaching style. His background includes extensive research and hands-on experience in various physics fields, enriching his lessons with real-world applications.

Education: Attiqulla holds advanced degrees in Physics and has undergone additional training in educational technology and online teaching methodologies. This combination of academic and practical expertise allows him to deliver high-quality education through EFA’s online platform.
