About

Javed Sahibzada

Profession: Javed Sahibzada is an experienced English instructor at Education For Afghanistan (EFA), holding a Master’s degree in English. He is dedicated to teaching English language and literature with a focus on helping students achieve fluency and a deep appreciation for the language.

What I Teach: Javed specializes in English instruction, covering areas such as grammar, writing, reading comprehension, and literature. His courses are designed to enhance students' language skills, from basic communication to advanced analysis of literary texts.

Teaching Philosophy: Javed’s teaching approach is centered on making English learning both practical and enjoyable. He believes in fostering a supportive environment where students are encouraged to engage with the language, develop their communication skills, and explore literary works.

Experience: With a strong background in both classroom and online teaching, Javed brings a wealth of experience to his role. He is known for his clear and structured teaching style, which helps students build confidence and achieve success in their English studies.

Education: Javed holds a Master’s degree in English and has completed additional training in education and online teaching strategies. His expertise in both the language and its pedagogy allows him to deliver high-quality education through EFA’s platform.
